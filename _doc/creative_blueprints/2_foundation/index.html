Foundation

Why this product exists

The Problem

Empty chairs. Scattered plans. Lost charges.

  • • No-shows drain revenue and burn chair time staff can't recover.
  • • Multi-visit treatment plans live in scattered notes between staff.
  • • Co-pays, balances, and insurance estimates sit in disconnected tools.
  • • The next clinician rarely has a clean picture of plan progress.
The Solution

One workspace. Plan, visit, bill — always linked.

  • → Schedule view with built-in confirmations and no-show flags.
  • → Per-patient treatment plans tracked step by step across visits.
  • → Patient ledger for charges, payments, balances, and estimates.
  • → One screen per workflow — reception, clinical, billing.
