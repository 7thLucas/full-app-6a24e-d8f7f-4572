# Product Overview

## What it is

A clinic operations app for dental practice staff. Front-desk coordinators, dental assistants, and office managers use it to manage patient appointments, treatment plans, and billing in one place — reducing no-shows and streamlining patient care.

## Who it's for

- **Front-desk coordinators** — schedule and reschedule appointments, confirm patients, handle check-in.
- **Dental assistants and hygienists** — view today's patients, see treatment plans, log treatment progress.
- **Office managers** — track billing, outstanding balances, and clinic throughput.

## Core problems it solves

- **No-shows drain revenue and chair time.** Patients forget appointments or fail to confirm; staff lose hours each week chasing them by phone.
- **Treatment plans live in scattered notes.** Multi-visit plans (crowns, root canals, ortho) are hard to track across staff and visits.
- **Billing is disjointed.** Co-pays, insurance estimates, and outstanding balances are tracked in separate tools, causing missed charges and slow collections.
- **Care continuity suffers** when the next person seeing the patient doesn't have a clean picture of plan progress.

## What it does

- **Appointment management** — daily/weekly schedule view, booking, rescheduling, confirmations, and no-show flags.
- **Treatment plans** — per-patient plan with ordered procedures, status per step, and visit linkage.
- **Billing** — per-patient ledger of charges, payments, balances, and insurance estimates.
- **Patient record** — single profile linking appointments, plan, and billing history.

## Strategic principles

- **Staff-first, not patient-facing.** Optimized for the people running the clinic.
- **One screen per workflow.** Reception, clinical, and billing each get a focused view — no context switching mid-task.
- **Reduce no-shows by default.** Confirmation and reminder workflows are built into scheduling, not bolted on.
- **Plan, visit, bill — always linked.** Every appointment ties back to a treatment plan step and a billing line.

## Out of scope (initially)

- Direct patient-facing portal or mobile patient app.
- Clinical imaging / X-ray storage.
- Full insurance claim adjudication (estimates only).
- Multi-clinic franchise management.

## Tone & brand

Calm, clinical, trustworthy. Clean typography, generous whitespace, healthcare-grade precision. Brand palette leans on a confident teal primary, deep navy for text, and a warm coral accent for alerts and key actions.
